#Strategy1: EMA
-When 9 EMA crossing 21 EMA and at the same time price is above VMAP,VWMA and Super trend -  take trade.
-When 9 EMA crossing 21 EMA and at the same time price is above VMAP,VWMA but below ST it may respect ST, once candle closes above ST - take a trade.
-When 9 EMA crossing 21 EMA and price is below VMAP,VWMA and Super trend, it respect all these - Take trade till VMAP,VWMA and Super trend.

#Strategy2: VWAP
